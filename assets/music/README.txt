BACKGROUND MUSIC — drop royalty-free tracks here (.mp3 / .m4a / .wav).
The editor picks one at random per clip and ducks it under the voice.
Leave this folder empty to render clips with no added music.

IMPORTANT — "trending" audio vs. background music (two different things):

1. TRENDING SOUNDS (the viral TikTok/Reels audio) are copyrighted and
   platform-licensed. You CANNOT legally download and bundle them — and if you
   did, the platforms would mute or block the video. The right way to use a
   trending sound is IN-APP at upload time: tap "Use sound" (TikTok) or the
   Trending tab (Reels). So: render your clip here with light/no music, then add
   the trending sound in-app if one fits. For business accounts, use TikTok's
   Commercial Music Library (cleared for commercial use).

2. BACKGROUND MUSIC (what goes in this folder) should be royalty-free / CC0 so
   your own uploads never get struck. Free, no-attribution sources (2026):
     - Pixabay Music        https://pixabay.com/music/        (CC0, no attribution)
     - Mixkit               https://mixkit.co/free-stock-music/
     - YouTube Audio Library  (in YouTube Studio — free, filter by "no attribution")
     - Uppbeat              https://uppbeat.io/   (free w/ attribution, or paid)
   Download 5-10 tracks that match your podcast's vibe and drop them here.

Tip: keep music subtle (config.yaml -> editor.music_volume, default 0.12).
