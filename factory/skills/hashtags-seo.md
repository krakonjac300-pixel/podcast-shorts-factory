# Skill: Hashtags & Discovery SEO

Helping the algorithm and search understand and route the clip.

- **Hashtag mix:** 3–6 total. One broad (#podcast), one medium niche (#businesspodcast), one
  specific/topical to the clip (#coldoutreach). Avoid 20-tag spam — it dilutes signal.
- **Spoken keywords matter:** platforms transcribe audio. Naturally saying the topic helps SEO.
- **Caption keywords:** include the core topic phrase early in the caption for search.
- **Platform nuances:**
  - *YouTube Shorts:* keyword-rich title + description; #Shorts; tags reinforce topic.
  - *TikTok:* a couple of trending + niche tags; SEO increasingly text-based — put keywords in caption.
  - *Instagram Reels:* topical tags + keyworded caption; alt text where possible.
- **Trend-jacking:** if a relevant trending sound/topic fits honestly, ride it — but never at
  the cost of the clip's clarity.
- **Consistency of niche** trains the algorithm on who to show you to. Don't whiplash topics.
