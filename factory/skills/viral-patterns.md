# Skill: Viral Patterns

Recurring shapes of clips that overperform. Use as a checklist when scoring moments.

- **The contrarian truth** — challenges a widely held belief with a credible reason.
- **The insider secret** — "what they don't tell you about X."
- **The vulnerable confession** — a real failure or fear, told plainly.
- **The mind-blowing fact** — a single counterintuitive data point.
- **The rant** — high-conviction, fast, emotional take on something relatable.
- **The mic-drop** — builds to one devastating closing line.
- **The teachable framework** — a crisp 2–3 step model the viewer can repeat.
- **The story with a twist** — expectation set, then flipped.
- **The "wait, what?" exchange** — a guest says something the host reacts to in real time.

**Avoid (these usually flop as clips):**
- Inside jokes, ad reads, logistics, "where can people find you" outros.
- Slow consensus agreement with no tension.
- Abstract talk with no example, number, or story.
- Anything requiring prior context from the episode.
