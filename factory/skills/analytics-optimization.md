# Skill: Analytics & Optimization

Reading performance and turning it into better clips next time.

- **North-star metrics:** average view duration / % watched and retention curve shape > raw
  views. Then shares (reach multiplier) and comments (engagement signal).
- **Read the retention curve:**
  - Big drop in first 3s → weak hook. Test colder opens.
  - Steady slope → fine; gentle is normal.
  - Mid-clip cliff → a slow/confusing section; tighten or cut that beat.
  - Spike/rewatch bump → people looped a moment; make more clips like it.
- **Compare predicted vs actual:** when the AI's virality score badly misses, note why — feed
  the correction back into selection.
- **Find patterns across winners:** topic, hook type, length, emotion, caption style. Double down
  on what repeats; retire what consistently underperforms.
- **One variable at a time:** change hook OR length OR style per batch so you can attribute results.
- **Post consistently** and give clips 48–72h before judging; short-form often has a slow burn.
- **Output:** a concise, specific learnings brief telling the Finder what to favor/avoid next run.
