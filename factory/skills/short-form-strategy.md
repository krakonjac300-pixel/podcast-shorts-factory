# Skill: Short-Form Strategy

How vertical short-form (Shorts / Reels / TikTok) actually wins.

- **Retention is everything.** The algorithm promotes watch-time % and rewatches, not likes.
  A 30s clip watched to 90% beats a 60s clip watched to 40%.
- **The first 3 seconds decide 80% of the outcome.** If the hook doesn't stop the scroll,
  nothing else matters. Open on the most surprising line, not the setup.
- **Self-contained.** A clip must make complete sense with zero outside context. Never start
  mid-reference ("like I said earlier…").
- **One idea per clip.** Don't bundle. One claim, one story, one payoff.
- **End on a loop or a question** so viewers rewatch or comment. Avoid limp endings ("…so yeah").
- **Ideal length:** 18–40s for talking-head clips. Go to 60s only if the story earns it.
- **Comment bait:** controversial-but-defensible takes, "agree?" moments, and small factual
  hooks people love to correct all drive the comments that boost reach.
- **Caption/title does half the work** — it sets the expectation the hook then pays off.
