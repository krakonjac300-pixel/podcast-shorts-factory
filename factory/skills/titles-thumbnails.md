# Skill: Titles & Covers

The packaging that wins (or loses) the click before a single second plays.

- **Title (≤ 60 chars that matter):** curiosity gap, bold claim, or stakes. The first 40
  characters are what most viewers actually see — load them.
- **Cover/thumbnail text (for Reels/TikTok covers):** 3–5 huge words capturing the single
  most intriguing idea. Should be readable at thumbnail size.
- **Faces with emotion** outperform neutral frames as covers — pick the most expressive moment.
- **Contrast & legibility:** big bold text, strong outline, high contrast with the background.
- **Don't duplicate** the spoken hook word-for-word — complement it to create a fuller promise.
- **Series branding:** a consistent cover layout/color makes a creator's clips instantly
  recognizable in the feed.
- **#Shorts** in the YouTube title/description helps classification for the Shorts shelf.
