# Skill: The Million-View Money Formula (reverse-engineered 2026-07-17)

Source: teardown of the money-clip niche's top channels (Caleb Hammer 902k
views/video, Graham Stephan 868k, Ramsey Highlights 287k) and a frame-by-frame +
transcript breakdown of a 1.35M-view exemplar ("Girlfriend Gets Caught Hiding
$15,000 DEBT", 42s). This is the grammar of money clips that hit MILLIONS.

## The 5-beat script (this is THE structure — find moments that have it)
1. **QUESTION-HOOK with stakes (0–2s).** The clip opens on a question that
   creates an instant open loop: "How much debt is your girlfriend in?" Never a
   statement of fact, never context.
2. **THE NUMBER, TO THE CENT (by ~5s).** "$15,586.97" beats "$15k". Precision
   reads as real and hits harder. The number must be SPOKEN early.
3. **ESCALATION LADDER.** Each beat worse than the last: 7 separate debts →
   savings is NEGATIVE → spent $1,000 more than she made. Stack the reveals;
   never plateau.
4. **THE EMOTIONAL TURN.** Money becomes a HUMAN conflict: betrayal, hiding it
   from a partner, shame, an ultimatum. The money is the trigger; the
   relationship/emotion is the story. (Million-view titles: "Girlfriend CAUGHT
   Hiding $15,000 DEBT", "'Become a Mother or I'm Filing for Divorce'",
   "Couple Breaks Down in Tears".)
5. **END UNRESOLVED.** The exemplar cuts on the trauma reveal, no resolution —
   comments finish the story. A tidy ending kills the comment section.

## Topics that hit millions (measured)
- Debt confrontations + hidden debt in relationships (the king)
- Absurd spending exposed ("$16,000 on AI credits", "spent $1,000 more than earned")
- Forbidden-knowledge money facts ("Bankruptcy wipes hospital bills overnight")
- Rich-people absurdity ("I rented a private jet", "$20M on YouTube")
- Archetype clashes (socialist vs capitalist tax fights, boss pays $0 salary)

## Edit grammar (what the 1.35M clip actually does)
- 100% FACES. Zero b-roll, zero stock, zero AI images. Tight punch-ins only.
- **Reaction shots are half the edit**: cut to the LISTENER's face during the
  other person's bombshell (the girlfriend's awkward smirk while her debts are
  read out IS the moment). When picking take windows, include the reaction.
- Real camera cut every ~3.5–4s + caption page every ~1.5s = constant change.
- Captions bottom-third, ALL-CAPS, 3–4 words/page, WHITE base + RED on every
  dollar amount and money word + YELLOW on emotional words. Two-color emphasis.
- Duration: money confrontations sustain 35–55s (longer than one-liner clips —
  the escalation ladder needs room). Single-beat moments still 20–40s.

## Titles (uploader)
EXACT number + relationship stake + a caught/exposed verb, ≤60 chars:
"Girlfriend CAUGHT Hiding $15,000 DEBT" / "She Spent $1,000 More Than She MADE".
Number in the first 30 chars. One emoji max, usually none.
